During a two month apprenticeship program in Bhuj, Gujurat, we worked on a project that was developing space saving furniture for lower income households. The project was facilitated by Hunnarshala, aimed to design furniture that would be mass produced by the local artisans.

The Tipping Point - A teaching aid game

Researchers at ATREE are studying the impacts of pressures and adaptive ability of socio-ecological systems in the Moyar Bhavani region in Tamil Nadu in order to understand the implications of these changes on communities living in the area. The Moyar Bhavani is characterized by a diverse ecological and human landscape that consists of two distinct areas: the predominantly agrarian Bhavani region and the forested Moyar landscape.
